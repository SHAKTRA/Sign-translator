Breaking Communication Barriers with Manos

Bridging the gap between hearing and deaf communities through real-time sign language translation

The Problem

Over 466 million people worldwide have disabling hearing loss, yet communication barriers persist between deaf and hearing communities. Traditional sign language interpretation is often:

  • Expensive and not always available
  • Limited to specific locations and times
  • Creates dependency on third-party interpreters
  • Reduces spontaneous communication opportunities
 Our Solution

Manos provides real-time, AI-powered sign language translation that:

  • Translates between 40+ signed and spoken languages instantly
  • Works offline - no internet required
  • Free and accessible to everyone
  • Empowers direct communication without intermediaries
